## Who We Are

didehLabs is an innovation lab focused on helping businesses identify and fix problems that negatively affect customer retention, revenue, visibility, and growth.

We are not a traditional agency, consultancy, or software company.

We build and operate intelligent systems that continuously discover businesses, review their online presence, identify issues, generate useful insights, and support our team in delivering solutions.

Our goal is simple:

Find problems businesses don't know they have, show them the evidence, and help them fix those problems at scale.

Their websites are slow.

## What We Do

At the core of didehLabs is an internal platform designed to identify opportunities and problems across many businesses.

### 1. Business Discovery (The Scout)

Our Scout system discovers businesses using publicly available business information and mapping platforms.

It searches for businesses within specific industries and locations and gathers relevant information for analysis.

This allows us to build targeted business lists that can be reviewed for opportunities and issues.

### 2. Problem Detection (The Engine)

Once businesses are discovered, our auditing system reviews their online presence.

It evaluates websites and identifies issues that may be affecting customer acquisition, retention, and revenue.

The system can detect:

* Slow-loading websites
* Broken images
* Missing website security
* Broken forms
* Missing calls-to-action (CTAs)
* Search visibility issues
* Performance problems
* User experience issues
* Website errors

Most importantly, we don't just identify problems—we explain how those problems may be impacting business outcomes.

### 3. Lead Prioritization

Not every issue has the same impact.

Our prioritization system helps us identify which businesses are likely to benefit most from intervention based on:

* Severity of detected issues
* Potential revenue impact
* Website quality
* Business visibility
* Opportunity size

This helps our team focus attention where the greatest value can be created.

### 4. Personalized Outreach

Using artificial intelligence and business-specific information, our platform generates personalized outreach messages tailored to each business.

Rather than sending generic templates, the system references actual findings and provides relevant insights supported by evidence.

The goal is to create meaningful conversations based on real business problems.

### 5. Reporting and Recommendations

For every audit, the platform generates reports that clearly explain:

* What is wrong
* Why it matters
* How it affects customers
* How it may affect revenue
* Recommended actions

This transforms complex findings into clear business insights.

### 6. Implementation and Improvement

Finding problems is only the beginning.

When businesses respond, our team works directly with them to implement solutions and manage improvements.

The platform helps us identify opportunities, generate insights, and streamline communication, but the relationship, strategy, and execution remain human.

Our objective is not simply to report problems but to create measurable business outcomes.
